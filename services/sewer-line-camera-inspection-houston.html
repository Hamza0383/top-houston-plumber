Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of a Sewer Line Camera Inspection?

The purpose of a Sewer Line Camera Inspection is to visually inspect your sewer lines to detect any blockages, cracks, or damage. This helps to diagnose and fix sewer line issues before they become major problems.

How long does a Sewer Line Camera Inspection take?

The length of a Sewer Line Camera Inspection can vary depending on the complexity of the issue and the length of the sewer lines. On average, an inspection can take anywhere from 30 minutes to several hours.

Do I need to be present for the inspection?

No, you do not need to be present for the inspection. Our team will arrive on site, complete the inspection, and provide a detailed report of our findings.
